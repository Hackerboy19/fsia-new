About the Pageant

Forever Miss India is unique in its concept — we reach pan India, starting with city winners, then state winners, and finally Forever Miss India is crowned. Every city gets representation.
It's one of the biggest pageants in the history of Indian beauty pageants, built on a unique and exclusive platform model. Every girl from anywhere in India can register.
The model is designed with maximum transparency. Every participant receives a lifetime membership of the FSIA community along with many other benefits including branding, media coverage, and Google visibility.
There are several participants from each city of India. The top 3 from every city compete at the state level, and the state-wise top 3 then compete for the national Forever Miss India 2026 title.
